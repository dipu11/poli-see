How to read the buildUp privacy policy

buildUp is a fitness-tracking app and social network. This visualization of the buildUp privacy policy is designed to help you understand how buildUp collects your personal data and how it can use or share your data. We'll give you a quic tutorial here.

In this visualization, you are in the center. Every other circle represents a category of your data that buildUp may collect.

Your data can be shared or changed. This is represented by arrows pointing to new circles representing your data after it has been shared or changed.

Hover over a circle with your mouse to select it and learn more about how your data can be shared, changed, or used. You can click on a circle to keep it selected. You can also hover over arrows in this way to learn more.

There are two rings in the display. The inner ring, labeled "buildUp," contains circles representing data that is held by buildUp. The outer ring, labeled "Third parties," contains circles representing data that is held by others.

Some data is identified as belonging to you. These circles are colored dark blue. Other data is unidentified, and these circles are gray.

Sometimes, buildUp describes what it can do with any of your data that it collects. This is represented by circles with a white background and a warning sign.
